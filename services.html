Coaching Options

Choose the option that best fits your needs. Both offerings draw on years of clinical experience and evidence‑based training methods to help you move better, feel stronger and perform at your best.

Online Coaching

Remote coaching gives you the flexibility to train on your own schedule with expert guidance wherever you live. I’ll design a customised programme based on your goals, current ability and equipment access, blending therapeutic exercise and strength training to advance you safely and effectively.

Throughout each month you’ll receive regular check‑ins, video feedback and modifications to keep you progressing. This option is ideal if you have recently finished physical therapy and want to continue building strength and confidence or if you simply prefer to train independently with ongoing support.

What’s included:

  • Initial assessment via video call and movement screen
  • Individualised programming delivered weekly
  • Weekly video analysis and feedback
  • Messaging support for questions and accountability
  • Progress tracking and programme adjustments

$400 / month

In‑Person Training

One‑on‑one training sessions are conducted in San Francisco and provide hands‑on coaching to help you rebuild strength, mobility and confidence. Each 60‑minute session is personalised to your objectives and includes movement assessments, corrective exercises and progressive strength work.

In‑person coaching is perfect if you value direct supervision and immediate feedback. Whether you’re returning to sport after injury, preparing for an event or simply want to feel your best, we’ll collaborate to ensure each session moves you closer to your goals.

What’s included:

  • Comprehensive movement analysis and goal setting
  • Personalised strength and mobility training
  • Hands‑on cueing and technique refinement
  • Progressive programming tailored to your lifestyle
  • Optional nutrition and lifestyle guidance

$160 / hour
