About Me

Hi, I'm Rehan Shaikh, a licensed physical therapist assistant (PTA) and certified strength and conditioning coach (CSCS). After earning my BS in Kinesiology and AAS in Physical Therapy Assisting, I founded Bridge the Gap Performance to help athletes and everyday movers transition safely from rehab to high performance.

My approach is holistic and evidence-based. I combine functional movement screens (SFMA), strength & conditioning principles, and nutrition coaching (PN Level 1) to create personalized programs that meet you where you are and take you where you want to go.

Credentials

  • Licensed Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA)
  • Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS)
  • PN Level 1 Nutrition Coach
  • SFMA Certified Practitioner
  • BS in Kinesiology
  • AAS in Physical Therapy Assisting

Philosophy

  • Holistic Approach – mind & body work together
  • Evidence-Based Practices – blending rehabilitation & performance science
  • Client-Centered Coaching – individualized plans built on trust
