About Your Coach

Empowering you to reclaim your strength and confidence with evidence‑based training and compassionate guidance.

Bridging Rehab & Performance

I’m a Licensed Physical Therapist Assistant with years of experience helping hundreds of clients transition from rehabilitation back to vibrant, pain‑free movement. My passion l ies in closing the gap between finishing physical therapy and returning to your favourite activities — whether that’s running, lifting, playing a sport or simply keeping up with your kids.

Throughout my career I’ve worked extensively with knee injuries such as ACL repairs, meniscus tears and patellar pain, as well as ankle, Achilles and shoulder issues. By blending therapeutic exercise with strength and conditioning principles, I’ll help you regain mobility, build resilience and elevate your performance.

  • Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS)
